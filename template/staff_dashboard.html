Scan and Detect

With the "Scan and Detect" feature, you can effortlessly scan both the front and back of any product packaging. The system uses advanced image recognition technology to automatically extract crucial details like the product name and expiry date. T hese details are then stored in a secure, easy-to-access database for future reference. This feature eliminates manual entry errors and saves time, helping you maintain an accurate record of all your products. You can also get notified when a product's expiry date is approaching, ensuring you never miss important updates.

Product Summary

The "Product Summary" feature offers a comprehensive overview of all the scanned products in your inventory. It provides detailed information, including the product name, expiry date, and the current status of each item (e.g., expired, about to expire, or within valid usage). This makes it easy to manage and track inventory, helping you stay organized and reduce waste. The feature is designed to display a clean, user-friendly interface that lets you quickly sort and search for specific products, giving you full control over your stock management process.
