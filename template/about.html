Welcome to Expiry Edge—your ultimate solution for smart stock management and expiry date tracking. Designed to streamline inventory control, Expiry Edge helps businesses manage their product stock efficiently while minimizing waste and saving valuable time. By scanning product names and expiry dates using a smart recognition system, Expiry Edge simplifies how you track and monitor inventory.

Our platform ensures that products nearing their expiration are detected early and highlighted, enabling you to take timely action. With automated reminders and clear notifications, businesses can stay ahead of potential losses, improve decision-making, and optimize stock rotation seamlessly.

At Expiry Edge, we are committed to creating a smarter, eco-conscious future by reducing product wastage through innovative technology. If you'd like to learn more or need support, we’re here to help. Together, let’s build a future of smart inventory management!
